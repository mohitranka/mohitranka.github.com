About

How I work

  • Clarity first: clear contracts, clear ownership, clear metrics.
  • Platform leverage: build one capability that saves hundreds of developer hours.
  • Measured outcomes: latency, error rates, adoption, and maintenance cost.
  • End-to-end ownership: coach engineers to own outcomes, not just code.

What I care about

Reliable systems, readable code, strong primitives (good APIs, observability, SLOs), and product sensibility in infrastructure work. I like work that’s strategic yet executable — problems that reward patience and craftsmanship.
